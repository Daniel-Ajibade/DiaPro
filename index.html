Understanding Your Diabetes

If you're reading this, you're in the right place. Let's quickly understand what type of diabetes you have:

Type 1 Diabetes

What it is: Your pancreas produces little or no insulin. This is usually diagnosed in childhood or early adulthood, but can occur at any age.

Common signs: Frequent urination, extreme thirst, constant hunger despite eating, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, and blurred vision.

‚úì DiaPro works effectively for Type 1 diabetes

Type 2 Diabetes

What it is: Your body doesn't use insulin properly (insulin resistance). This is the most common type, usually developing after age 45, but increasingly affecting younger people.

Common signs: Increased thirst and urination, increased hunger, fatigue, blurred vision, slow-healing sores, frequent infections, and tingling in hands or feet.
